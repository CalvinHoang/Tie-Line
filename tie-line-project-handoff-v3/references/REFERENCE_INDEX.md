## MATS2008 Notes

`MATS2008 Notes.pdf` is the supplied course-notes reference used to establish the initial scientific content taxonomy.

Relevant sections visible in the supplied document:

- pages 1–2: ternary compatibility triangles, invariant reasoning, isothermal sections, and crystallisation paths;
- pages 4–12: binary diagram classifications, invariant-reaction schematics, compounds, solubility, and related construction families;
- pages 13–17: real binary system examples for future scientific golden fixtures.

The notes guide content and validation. Executable rules remain in the versioned Formal Rules documents.
